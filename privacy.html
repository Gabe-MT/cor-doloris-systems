Privacy Policy

Cor Doloris Systems uses SMS messaging for account notifications, system updates, development testing, and related service communications.

Phone numbers collected for SMS messaging are used only for sending requested or consented-to text messages. Cor Doloris Systems does not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply.

Recipients may reply STOP at any time to opt out of SMS messages. Recipients may reply HELP for assistance.

For questions, contact Cor Doloris Systems at your registered support contact.
