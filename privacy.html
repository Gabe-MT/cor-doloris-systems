Privacy Policy

Cor Doloris Systems uses SMS messaging to provide account notifications, onboarding confirmations, service updates, and system alerts to users who have consented to receive them.

Mobile phone numbers, SMS consent records, and related messaging information are used only to provide the requested messaging service. Mobile information and SMS consent are not sold, rented, or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

Recipients may reply STOP at any time to opt out of SMS messages. Recipients may reply HELP for assistance.

For questions regarding this policy, contact Cor Doloris Systems through the contact information associated with the service.
