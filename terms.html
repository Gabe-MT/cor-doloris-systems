SMS Terms & Conditions

Users may enroll in the Cor Doloris Systems SMS notification program by texting START to the phone number displayed on the Cor Doloris Systems website.

Users may opt out at any time by replying STOP. After opting out, users may receive one final message confirming that they have been unsubscribed.
